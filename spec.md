# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Build a professional beauty salon website for Marisol's Beauty Salon with online booking system, service showcase, gallery, and contact information.

**Planned changes:**
- Create hero section with headline, subheadline, and CTA buttons (Book Appointment scroll, Call Now click-to-call)
- Add About Us section introducing Marisol with 4.6-star rating
- Build Services section with 5 service cards (Haircut & Styling, Hair Coloring, Hair Treatments, Blow Dry, Beauty Services)
- Implement online booking form with backend storage (Full Name, Phone, Email, Service dropdown, Date, Time)
- Create Gallery section with grid layout for before/after transformations and salon interior images
- Add Reviews section displaying 4.6-star Google rating and 3-5 client testimonials
- Build Contact section with address, click-to-call phone, embedded Google Map, and business hours
- Apply rose gold, beige, white, and black color palette throughout
- Ensure full mobile responsiveness and optimized images

**User-visible outcome:** A clean, elegant salon website where visitors can view services, browse gallery photos, read reviews, and submit booking requests through an online form. The site includes click-to-call functionality and an embedded map for easy contact.
